Activities Completed

During Lab 02, the team conducted IT opportunity scanning to identify potential semester project ideas. We brainstormed multiple problems faced by university students and evaluated each idea based on the NUF (New, Useful, Feasible) scoring framework.

The team identified six potential opportunities:

1. Smart Assignment Reminder App
2. Campus Study Group Finder
3. QR Code Attendance System
4. Smart Campus Navigation App
5. Lost and Found Management Platform
6. Student Budget Tracker

For each idea, we defined the target users, identified the problem area, analyzed existing alternatives, proposed possible IT solutions, and assessed whether the project could be completed within one semester.

We created an opportunity scoring matrix and calculated the total score and ranking for each idea.

Selected Opportunity


The team selected the Smart Assignment Reminder App as the semester project opportunity.

Why It Was Selected

The selected project achieved one of the highest NUF scores.
